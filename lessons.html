Algebra Math Lessons

Units 1 & 8 – Linear Functions & Geometry

Learn about linear graphs, slope-intercept form, point-slope form, arithmetic sequences, perpendicular lines, distance, and midpoint.

Units 2 & 3 – Linear Inequalities & Radicals

Explore systems of linear inequalities, simplified radicals, solutions, and how to identify rational and irrational numbers.

Unit 4 – Quadratics

Dive into solving quadratic equations, the quadratic formula, vertex form, and graphing parabolas.

Units 5 & 6 – Exponentials

Understand geometric sequences, modeling growth and decay, graphing exponential functions, and solving exponential equations.

Unit 7 – Statistics

Learn about mean, median, box plots, the 5-number summary, outliers, correlation, and causation.
