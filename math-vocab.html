Math Vocabulary

Key algebra terms to boost your understanding

Expressions & Equations

Variable

A symbol that represents a number.

Expression

Combination of numbers, variables, and operations.

Equation

A statement showing two expressions are equal.

Term

Part of an expression separated by + or - signs.

Coefficient

The number multiplying a variable.

Constant

A value that does not change.

Operations & Properties

Distributive Property

a(b + c) = ab + ac.

Inverse Operation

Operations that undo each other.

Simplify

Reduce an expression to its simplest form.

Evaluate

Find the value of an expression.

Exponents & Roots

Exponent

Shows how many times a base is multiplied.

Base

The number used in repeated multiplication.

Exponent Rules

Guidelines to simplify powers.

Radical

An expression with a root symbol.

Square Root

Value that gives the original number when squared.

Functions & Graphing

Function

Each input has one output.

Domain

All possible input values.

Range

All possible output values.

Slope

Steepness of a line (rise/run).

Y-Intercept

Where the graph crosses the y-axis.

X-Intercept

Where the graph crosses the x-axis.

Equations & Inequalities

Linear Equation

Graphs a straight line.

Quadratic Equation

Has the form ax¬≤ + bx + c = 0.

Inequality

Compares values using <, >, ‚â§, ‚â•.

Solution

Value(s) that make an equation true.

Number Types

Integer

All whole numbers and their opposites.

Natural Numbers

Counting numbers starting at 1.

Whole Numbers

Natural numbers including 0.

Rational Number

Can be expressed as a fraction.

Irrational Number

Cannot be expressed as a fraction.

Real Numbers

All rational and irrational numbers.

Additional Terms

Graph

Visual representation of data.

System of Equations

Multiple equations with same variables.

Substitution

Replace a variable with a value.

Elimination

Add/subtract equations to eliminate a variable.

Standard Form

Written as Ax + By = C.

Factor

Numbers/expressions multiplied to get a product.

GCF

Greatest factor shared by terms.

LCM

Smallest multiple two numbers share.

Order of Operations

PEMDAS: evaluate in proper sequence.
