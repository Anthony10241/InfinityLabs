Units 5 & 6 – Exponentials

This unit focuses on exponential functions and their applications. You'll learn to identify geometric sequences, model exponential growth and decay, and graph exponential equations. You'll also develop strategies for solving exponential equations, both graphically and algebraically.

Question: Is the equation \( y = 2 \cdot (1.5)^x \) modeling growth or decay? Explain your reasoning.
